Cheesy Pepperoni Pizza

Crispy golden crust topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey melted mozzarella, and perfectly spiced pepperoni - this homemade pizza is a crowd-pleaser for any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
    • 2 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
    • 1.5 cups warm water
    • 3-4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
  • Pizza Toppings
    • 1 cup pizza sauce
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 100g (about 3.5 oz) pepperoni slices
    • 1 tsp dried oregano (optional)
    • Olive oil for brushing

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Stir in olive oil, salt, and 2 cups of the flour. Mix well.
  3. Gradually add the remaining flour until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 6-8 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-1.5 hours or until doubled in size.
  6. Preheat oven
  7. Punch down the dough and roll it out into your desired pizza shape on a floured surface.
  8. Transfer to a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
