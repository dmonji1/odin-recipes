Sweet and Moist Carrot Cake

Soft, spiced cake made with freshly grated carrots, layered with creamy frosting, and finished with a hint of warm cinnamon - this carrot cake is rich, comforting, and perfect for any celebration.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Carrots
  • Baking Soda
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans (or line with parchment paper).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. In a large bowl, beat together granulated sugar, brown sugar, and oil until combined.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined.
  6. Fold in grated carrots, nuts, and pineapple (if using).
  7. Divide the batter evenly between the cake pans and smooth the tops.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Let cakes cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  10. To make the frosting, beat cream cheese and butter until smooth. Gradually add powdered sugar, then vanilla, beating until fluffy.
  11. Frost the cooled cake layers and enjoy!
